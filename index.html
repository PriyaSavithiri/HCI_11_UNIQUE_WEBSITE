Thwarting Online Exam Malpractice

Deter.Detect.Prevent

Overview

Problem and solution

Abstract

The project aims to provide a safe and user-friendly environment to take exams online to various locations for those who don't have access to campus. The project employs various machine learning models to reduce the burden on the admin to detect malpractice by the students on a large scale. The model verifies and authenticates the candidates taking the exam and continuously checks for any malpractices. This is done by analysing video and audio that the model processes to verify the integrity of the student.


The problem In Online Proctoring,

  • To detect verbal assistance from another person in the same room or remotely via a phone call.
  • In online exams, cheating masterminds often use hard drives, USBs, Micro SDs and other external smart devices that are easy to conceal and challenging to detect. Before or during the test, test-takers plug them for easy use, making them standard accessories to online cheating.
  • Even after all possible preventions we done before conducting an exam , some might still cheat on the exams . Group of people could discuss and write the same answers or share the questions through Whatsapp and so. Therefore , we are going to detect the answer patterns and find if there are any abnormalities.
